Chleb's Classic Cars

From the Beginnings

It all started when my father purchased a 1966 Ford Mustang for my sister to drive to school. Then my brother drove it and lastly me. As I grew and entered HS I became hooked on the cars of my youth and started learning from others how to do body work, fix engines, weld, etc. Before long I bought my first car, restored it and sold it for a nice profit. So, I used the money to buy another and the cycle continued until I opened my shop. The rest is history as they say. Stop by anytime, we would love to talk classic cars with you!
